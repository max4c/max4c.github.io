Why Choose Weblog As The Name?

December 2023, Provo

When building this site, I couldn't decide if I wanted to have the public repository of my ideas to have the name: notes, essays, ideas, posts, articles, or blog. As this site ,and a large part of my life, is focused on doing only what is necessary, I chatted with GPT-4 to see which name would work best when the only consistent parts of each entry would be the date and title. I came to find out that the word blog is a truncation of the word weblog, similar to a ship's log where daily occurrences or thoughts are recorded.

As this is the web,

and here is a chronological list of my ideas,

I landed on weblog.
